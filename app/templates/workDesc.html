How Does It Work

The Degree Planner is a website that allows student of the University of the West Indies to plan their degree path. The user will first be ask to login into our system if they have an account.

other wise they will be able to sign up for an account.

The system works by the using a large database with tables that are all normalized and are connected by relationships. The information in the database are based extensively on the handbooks provided by UWI, also all incorrect information and missing data from the handbooks were manually added to the database. The website have different pages for the different reasons a User would like to use our planner. These include but are not limited to: Generation of plan, search, and Gpa calculation.

the search functionalities allows a user to search for information on a desired category within our systems capabilities. it searches through our systems exstensive database and gives accurate information on the category of search based on the information directly from the handbook, easing the pain of university students whom just want to easily search for background information on a course they want to do.

The generation of plan feature works by making the user choose the programmes they want to do,however only valid combinations will be processed. The user will also enter their prefference and this information will be used to determine/ recommend electives to the user in the generation of plans. Finally Gpa calculationis done by getting the letter grades from the user, the system will then use a custom algorithm based on the formula for calculation of gpa done at UWI to determine the user's Gpa.
